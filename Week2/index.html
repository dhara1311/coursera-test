Indian Food Dishes

DalBatti

Dal baati is an Indian dish of dal (lentils) and baati (hard wheat rolls). Wheat flour is kneaded with little bit of salt, dahi (yogurt) and water. Tennis ball-sized round balls of this dough are cooked in a well-heated traditional oven.

Samosa

A samosa is a fried or baked pastry with a savoury filling, such as spiced potatoes, onions, peas, cheese, beef and other meats, or lentils. It may take different forms, including triangular, cone, or half-moon shapes, depending on the region.The Indian style, often accompanied by a chutney.

Basundi

It is a sweetened condensed milk made by boiling milk on low heat until the milk is reduced by half. In North India, a similar dish goes by the name rabri. It is a dessert originating from the eastern regions of the Indian subcontinent, possibly Bengal.
